Book a driving theory test

Book a theory test for a:

  • car
  • motorcycle
  • lorry, bus or coach

You can also:

  • change the time or date of a theory test you've booked but haven't yet taken
  • cancel a theory test you've booked but haven't yet taken

This service is for England, Scotland and Wales. There's a different service to book a theory test in Northern Ireland.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg)

Start now

Before you start

You will need:

  • the UK driving licence number for the person taking the driving theory test
  • an email address - without one, you must book the theory test by phone
  • a credit or debit card to pay for the theory test

How much theory tests cost

Car £23
Motorbike £23

Lorry or bus

part 1a (multiple-choice questions) £26
part 1b (hazard perception) £11
part 2 (case studies) £23

When you don't need a theory test

You don’t need to take a theory test before you book a:

  • taxi driving test
  • tractor driving test

You also don’t need to take a theory test if you're taking a driving test to upgrade:

  • an automatic car licence to a manual car licence
  • from category A1 (small motorcycle) to category A2 (medium motorcycle) if you’ve had the licence for at least 2 years
  • from category C1 (medium vehicles) to category C (large vehicles) unless you got your C1 entitlement from passing a car driving test
  • from category D1 (minibus) to category D (bus)
  • any licence to be able to tow a trailer

Help with booking a test

Contact the Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVA) to get help booking your theory test, or book if you don’t have an email address.
