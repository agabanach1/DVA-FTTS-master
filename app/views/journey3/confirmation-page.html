At the test centre

All test centres are security monitored.

Personal items are not allowed in the testing room. They must be placed in the locker provided. These items include:

  • mobile phones or pagers
  • earpieces or headphones
  • laptops or hand-held computers
  • personal digital assistants (PDAs)
  • other electronic devices
  • handbags, purses or wallets
  • hats, bags or coats
  • books or revision notes
  • dictionaries
  • paper or pens
  • watches
  • food or drink

If a candidate is found to have any of these items in the testing room:

  • the test will be stopped and voided
  • they will be asked to leave
  • the test fee will not be refunded

Waiting facilities

There are no waiting facilities for any adults or children accompanying candidates.

Moving or cancelling the test

Theory tests can be moved or cancelled up to 3 days before without penalty. If this booking is changed or cancelled after 25 February 2020 no refund will be made.
