Do you need support while taking the theory test?

Support is available during the theory test for people who have a disability, health condition or difficulty reading.

Depending on the type of test, this might include:

  • an English or Welsh voiceover
  • use of British Sign Language
  • extra time to complete the test
  • someone reading or rewording the questions

If you ask for support, we might need you to provide evidence, such as a letter from a doctor or teacher.

You can get more information about theory test support.
