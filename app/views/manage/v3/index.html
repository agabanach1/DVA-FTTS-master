Change your theory test appointment

Use this service to:

  • find an earlier driving theory test
  • move your theory test to a later date
  • change which test centre you take your test at

This service is currently free because of coronavirus (COVID-19).

When you must not go to your test

You must not go to your test if:

  • you or someone you live with has coronavirus symptoms
  • you’ve been told by the NHS Test and Trace service that you’ve been in contact with a person who has coronavirus
  • you’re self-isolating because you recently entered the UK
