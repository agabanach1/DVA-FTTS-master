Use of cookies on this service

This service puts small files (known as 'cookies') onto your computer to collect information about how you use the service.

Cookies are used to remember the notifications you've seen so that we don't show them to you again. They are not used to identify you personally.

You will normally see a message before a cookie is stored on your computer.

Cookies used on this service

This service uses cookie types that allow the system to work securely and consistently. These are:

  • service application cookies
  • analytics cookies
  • third-party cookies

Service application cookies

This service sets a number of cookies to remember what you have done on the site and what messages you have seen.

You may see a pop-up cookies message when you first visit the service. We'll store a cookie so that your computer knows you have seen it and knows not to show it again.

Name Purpose Expires
cm-user-preferences Stores your acknowledgement of cookie use on this service 1 year

Analytics cookies

We use Google Analytics software to collect information about how you use the service. This helps us make the service better for its users. Google Analytics stores information about:

  • the service pages you visit
  • how long you spend on each page
  • how you got to the site
  • what you click on while you’re visiting the site

Google doesn't collect or store your personal information, for example your name or address, so this information can’t be used to identify who you are. We don’t allow Google to use or share our analytics data.

Google Analytics uses these cookies:
